About The Methodology

Venture Oracle uses advanced predictive modeling trained on thousands of historical startup funding rounds to determine the likelihood of a company surviving, being acquired, or going public versus closing down.

Our model evaluates factors such as total funding raised, average round sizes, funding duration, and industry categories to provide an actionable probability score.

How It Works

1. Enter the specific metrics of the target company.

2. The data is processed and encoded using our proprietary pipeline.

3. A pre-trained machine learning model analyzes the features and outputs a survival probability.
